Chapter 2:

At the start of the chapter the guests are all in a small crowd at the station where the train dropped them off. All of them leave with a taxi for Solider Island except for Vera Claythorne and Philip Lombard. Vera has been called in as a stand in secretary for Mr. Owen. They engage in idle talk as they wait for the next guests to arrive in the next train. The guest to arrive on the train is General Macarthur who quickly scans Mr. Lombard and comes to the conclusion that hes a good looking fellow but something is wrong about him. The guests then went onto the small boat that would take them to the island. As they are about to leave, Anthony Marston drives up to the meet up point.
